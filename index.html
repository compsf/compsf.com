About Us

Real IT. Real People. Built for Growing Businesses.

At CompSF, we believe IT should support your business, not slow it down.

That's why we take a structured, hands-on approach. We start by understanding how your business actually runs, then assess your entire environment, identify risks, and build a clear plan to improve it.

No guesswork. No band-aid fixes. Just a system that works.

Based in the Bay Area, we work closely with our clients, on-site and remotely, to organize, secure, and maintain everything their business depends on.

Because when your IT is done right, you don't have to think about it at all.
